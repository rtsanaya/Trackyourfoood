What is Western Food?

Western food is characterized by its use of ingredients such as meat. dairy products, grains, and vegetables, as well as techniques such as baking, grilling, and roasting.

TOP 10 WESTERN FOOD you should try!

1. Steak

Steak is a cut of beef that is grilled or fried, usually served with fries, vegetables, and sauce. In Indonesia, steak is popular in various restaurants, from high-end to more casual eateries. Some restaurants even serve steak with flavors adapted to local tastes.

2. Burger

A burger consists of a round bun filled with minced beef, chicken, or fish, along with vegetables like lettuce, tomatoes, and onions, and sauces such as mayonnaise and ketchup. Burgers are widely popular in Indonesia, both from international fast-food chains and local outlets.

3. Pizza

Pizza is a flatbread topped with ingredients like tomato sauce, cheese, meat, and vegetables. In Indonesia, pizza is very popular, and there are local variations that use ingredients such as "ayam balado" (spicy chicken) or "rendang" as toppings.

4. Fish and Chips

This dish consists of fish fried in batter and served with fries. Fish and chips are popular in restaurants that serve British-style food but can also be found in various international restaurants across Indonesia.

5. French Fries

French fries are a popular snack worldwide, including in Indonesia. They are usually served as a side dish for burgers, steaks, or other fast food items. French fries are loved by people of all ages in Indonesia.

6. Sandwich

A sandwich is bread filled with meat, cheese, vegetables, and sauces. In Indonesia, sandwiches are popular as a quick breakfast or lunch option. They come in various forms, from simple to more elaborate ones with a variety of fillings.

7. Salad

Salad is a mix of raw vegetables such as lettuce, tomatoes, carrots, and cucumbers, served with dressing like vinaigrette or mayonnaise. In Indonesia, salads are especially popular among those looking for healthy or diet-friendly food.

8. Pasta

Pasta includes various dishes made from wheat flour, originating from Italy, such as spaghetti, fettuccine, and lasagna. These dishes are often served with sauces like bolognese, carbonara, or pesto. Many restaurants in Indonesia offer pasta dishes that are adapted to local tastes.

9. Barbecue

BBQ consists of grilled or roasted meat with a special barbecue sauce. In Indonesia, BBQ is often served at family gatherings or parties, and dedicated BBQ restaurants are becoming increasingly common.

10. Pancakes

Pancakes are thin, flat cakes usually served with maple syrup, honey, or fruits. Pancakes are commonly found in restaurants or cafes serving Western-style breakfasts in Indonesia, often with a local twist such as using palm sugar or coconut.
